FAQ

Does the app score Mexican Train and Chickenfoot?
Yes—automatic pip counting and round tracking works great for both.

Can I use it for other round-based games?
Definitely. You can track any game that has players and rounds.

Camera isn’t counting accurately?
Try brighter lighting and keep tiles in frame. You can always edit scores manually.
